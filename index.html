ⓘ Business Overview — EventyON
▼
EventyON is a corporate event management and catering business. Revenue stabilised at ₽1,425.9 mln in 2024–2025. Q1 2026 came in at ₽280.6 mln — down 7% vs Q1 2025 (₽302.4 mln), with margin compressing from 22.0% to 15.3% as costs held flat while revenue declined.

Founded in 2012, EventyON grew from ₽7.0 mln in its first partial year to ₽467.9 mln in 2019, then expanded sharply: revenue crossed ₽1,000 mln in 2023 and held at ₽1,425.9 mln in both 2024 and 2025. The business specialises in corporate event management, catering, and entertainment services.

2024 delivered the highest EBIT on record at ₽359.9 mln (25.3% margin). 2025 was near-identical in revenue but margin compressed to 22.9% as salary costs outpaced growth.

Q1 2026 vs Q1 2025: Revenue ₽280.6 mln vs ₽302.4 mln (−7%). Costs ₽237.6 mln vs ₽236.0 mln (+1%). Net profit ₽43.0 mln vs ₽66.4 mln (−35%). Margin 15.3% vs 22.0% (−6.7pp). The cost base is essentially flat year-on-year — the entire margin compression comes from the revenue shortfall.

April 2026 adds ₽42.8 mln revenue at ₽18.8 mln profit (43.9% margin) — a strong month that brings YTD to ₽323.4 mln / ₽61.8 mln net.
